Raghu Engineering Institution, located in Visakhapatnam is
a private institute established in the year 2001.which was
established by "Raghu Kalidindi".REC received autonomous
status by UGC and is affiliated with the Jawaharlal Nehru
Technological University. REC is approved and accredited
by NAAC by ‘A’ grade and NBA. The college is known for its
UG and PG courses in engineering.
